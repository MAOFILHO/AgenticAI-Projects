# Management Certification of Financial Reports

**Citation:** Sarbanes-Oxley Act § 302  
**Authority:** SEC  

## Summary

Principal officers must personally certify the accuracy of financial reports and the effectiveness of disclosure controls.

## Key Requirements

- CEO and CFO must certify each periodic report's accuracy and completeness.
- Certify that disclosure controls and procedures are designed and evaluated.
- Disclose any significant deficiencies and acts of fraud to auditors and the audit committee.

## Detection Indicators

- Disclosure-control gaps that could permit material misstatement.
- Override of controls by management.

## Enforcement

Certifying a non-compliant report knowingly: fines up to $5,000,000 and imprisonment up to 20 years.
