# Access Controls & Privileged Access

**Citation:** FFIEC IT Examination Handbook — Information Security  
**Authority:** FFIEC  

## Summary

Institutions must control, monitor, and periodically review access — especially privileged access — to information systems.

## Key Requirements

- Apply least-privilege and need-to-know to all access grants.
- Review privileged access at least quarterly.
- Promptly revoke access upon role change or termination.
- Monitor for anomalous authentication (failed-login bursts, impossible travel).

## Detection Indicators

- Terminated employee account still active after offboarding.
- Burst of failed logins followed by success.
- Privileged access retained beyond the period of need.

## Enforcement

Information-security examination findings; heightened supervisory attention.
