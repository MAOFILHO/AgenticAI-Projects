# Third-Party Risk Management

**Citation:** OCC Bulletin 2020-10  
**Authority:** OCC  

## Summary

Banks must manage risks from third-party relationships across the full lifecycle, especially for critical vendors.

## Key Requirements

- Conduct due diligence on critical vendors before engagement.
- Scope vendor access to least privilege and the contracted purpose.
- Monitor vendor activity and performance throughout the relationship.
- Revoke vendor access promptly when no longer needed.

## Detection Indicators

- Vendor accessing systems outside its contracted scope.
- Critical vendor without documented due diligence.
- Vendor access not revoked after engagement end.

## Enforcement

Supervisory findings; OCC enforcement; operational and reputational risk.
