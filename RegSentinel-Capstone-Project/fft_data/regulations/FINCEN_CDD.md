# Customer Due Diligence (CDD) & Beneficial Ownership

**Citation:** 31 CFR § 1010.230  
**Authority:** FinCEN  

## Summary

Banks must identify and verify the beneficial owners of legal-entity customers and conduct ongoing due diligence.

## Key Requirements

- Identify each individual owning 25% or more of a legal-entity customer, plus one control person.
- Develop a customer risk profile and conduct ongoing monitoring to identify and report suspicious activity.
- Update customer information on a risk basis.

## Detection Indicators

- High-risk legal entity without documented beneficial ownership.
- Customer risk profile inconsistent with observed transaction behaviour.
- Newly-formed entity with immediate high-value international activity.

## Enforcement

BSA enforcement actions; consent orders; civil money penalties.
