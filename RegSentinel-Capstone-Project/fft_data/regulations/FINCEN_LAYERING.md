# Money Laundering Typologies — Layering

**Citation:** FinCEN Advisory (typology guidance)  
**Authority:** FinCEN  

## Summary

Layering separates illicit proceeds from their source through complex chains of transactions to obscure the audit trail.

## Key Requirements

- Monitor for rapid movement of funds in and out of accounts.
- Scrutinise wires to or from high-risk jurisdictions.
- Investigate funnel accounts that receive then immediately disperse funds.

## Detection Indicators

- Large wire-in followed within days by multiple smaller international wire-outs.
- Same-day in-and-out of round-dollar amounts.
- Wires to unrelated third parties shortly after large deposits.

## Enforcement

Layering activity supports SAR filing and may trigger Special Investigation Unit review.
