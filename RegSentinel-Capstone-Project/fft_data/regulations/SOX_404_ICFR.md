# Internal Control over Financial Reporting (ICFR)

**Citation:** Sarbanes-Oxley Act § 404  
**Authority:** SEC / PCAOB  

## Summary

Management and the external auditor must assess and report on the adequacy of internal control over financial reporting.

## Key Requirements

- Maintain segregation of duties — no individual may both initiate and approve a financial transaction.
- Require formal change management: every change to a financial system needs a ticket and independent authorization.
- Provision access on a least-privilege basis with periodic review.
- Document and test key controls annually.

## Detection Indicators

- Self-approved transactions (segregation-of-duties violation).
- Production changes to financial systems without an approved change ticket.
- Privileged access granted without authorization.

## Enforcement

Material weakness disclosure; restatement risk; SEC enforcement; investor and reputational impact.
