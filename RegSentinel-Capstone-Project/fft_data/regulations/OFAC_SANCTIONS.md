# OFAC Sanctions Screening

**Citation:** 31 CFR Chapter V  
**Authority:** Treasury — OFAC  

## Summary

Banks must screen transactions and customers against OFAC sanctions lists and block prohibited dealings.

## Key Requirements

- Screen customers and counterparties against the SDN (Specially Designated Nationals) list.
- Block or reject transactions involving sanctioned parties or jurisdictions.
- Report blocked/rejected transactions to OFAC.
- Maintain a risk-based sanctions compliance program.

## Detection Indicators

- Wires to or from comprehensively sanctioned jurisdictions.
- Counterparties matching SDN-list entities.
- Attempts to disguise the ultimate beneficiary of an international transfer.

## Enforcement

Strict-liability civil penalties (potentially millions per violation); criminal penalties for willful breaches.
