# Currency Transaction Report (CTR)

**Citation:** 31 CFR § 1010.311  
**Authority:** FinCEN — Bank Secrecy Act  
**Form:** FinCEN Form 112  

## Summary

Financial institutions must report currency transactions exceeding $10,000 in a single business day.

## Key Requirements

- File a CTR for every transaction in currency of more than $10,000 by, through, or to the institution on any one business day.
- Aggregate multiple currency transactions when the institution knows they are by or on behalf of the same person.
- File within 15 calendar days of the transaction.
- Retain CTR records for five years.

## Detection Indicators

- Single cash deposit or withdrawal above $10,000.
- Multiple same-day cash transactions by one customer totalling more than $10,000 across branches or channels.

## Enforcement

Civil penalties up to $25,000 per violation; willful violations may carry criminal penalties.
