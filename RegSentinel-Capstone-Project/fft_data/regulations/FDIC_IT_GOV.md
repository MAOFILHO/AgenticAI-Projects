# IT Governance

**Citation:** FDIC RMS Manual of Examination Policies — Section 4.5  
**Authority:** FDIC  

## Summary

Examined institutions must maintain documented IT governance with board oversight and risk-based controls.

## Key Requirements

- Establish board-level oversight of IT risk.
- Perform annual vendor risk assessment for critical third-party providers.
- Review privileged access at least quarterly.
- Require pre-approval for changes outside the documented change window.

## Detection Indicators

- No documented board oversight of IT risk.
- Critical vendor without a current risk assessment.
- Unreviewed privileged access accumulation.
