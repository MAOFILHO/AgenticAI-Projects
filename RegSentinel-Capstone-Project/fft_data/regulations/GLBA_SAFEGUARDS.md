# GLBA Safeguards Rule

**Citation:** Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act / FTC 16 CFR Part 314  
**Authority:** FTC / Interagency  

## Summary

Financial institutions must protect the security and confidentiality of customer information.

## Key Requirements

- Maintain a written information security program.
- Encrypt customer data in transit and at rest.
- Apply data-loss-prevention (DLP) controls to PII exports.
- Limit and log access to customer information.

## Detection Indicators

- Large PII export without DLP approval.
- Customer data accessed beyond business need.
- Unencrypted transmission of customer information.

## Enforcement

FTC / interagency enforcement; civil penalties; mandatory breach notification obligations.
